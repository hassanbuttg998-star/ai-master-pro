Inside FRAZX Lab

Welcome to FRAZX, an elite-level AI Prompt Engineering laboratory designed for creators who demand cinematic perfection. This isn't just a website; it's a bridge between human imagination and neural network execution.

Current Arsenal (Tools)

  • Cinematic Vision: Our flagship engine that converts raw ideas into high-fidelity 8K prompts. It focuses on lighting, camera angles, and textures.
  • Portrait Transformer: Specialized in human/character design, offering styles from Cyberpunk to Viking and Pixar-3D.

Future Roadmap (Coming Soon)
